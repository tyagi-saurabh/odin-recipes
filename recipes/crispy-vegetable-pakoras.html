Crispy Vegetable Pakoras

Description

Crispy Vegetable Pakoras are a popular Indian snack made with assorted vegetables dipped in a spiced gram flour batter and deep-fried until golden brown.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Assorted vegetables (potatoes, onions, spinach, cauliflower), sliced thinly
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin seeds, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually to make a smooth batter of medium consistency.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Dip the sliced vegetables into the batter, ensuring they are well coated.
  5. Carefully drop the battered vegetables into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove pakoras with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  7. Serve hot with mint chutney or ketchup.
