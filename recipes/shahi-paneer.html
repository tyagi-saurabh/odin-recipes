Shahi Paneer

Description

Shahi Paneer is a rich and creamy Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a flavorful gravy of nuts, spices, and cream.

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts
  • 1/4 cup almonds
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Soak cashew nuts and almonds in warm water for 15 minutes, then blend into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pan, add chopped onions, and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  4. Stir in the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add the nut paste, yogurt, garam masala, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes on low heat.
  6. Add the paneer cubes and gently mix to coat them with the gravy. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with cilantro before serving.
