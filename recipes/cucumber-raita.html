Cucumber Raita

Description

Cucumber Raita is a refreshing Indian side dish made with yogurt and grated cucumber, perfect for cooling down spicy meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 medium cucumber, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth.
  2. Add the grated cucumber and mix well.
  3. Stir in cumin powder and salt to taste.
  4. Garnish with chopped cilantro before serving.
